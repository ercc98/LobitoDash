Lobito Dash: Wolf Runner
Last updated: June 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Lobito Dash: Wolf Runner ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. The App is developed and published by Ernesto Refugio Cabrera Cerón ("the Developer"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account or to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or postal address to play the App. However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device & usage data — device model, operating system version, language, app version, and in-app events (such as runs started, scores, and crashes).
  • Identifiers — advertising identifiers (e.g., the Android Advertising ID) and app-instance identifiers used for analytics and advertising.
  • Approximate location — derived from your IP address by advertising and analytics services (we do not collect precise GPS location).

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — to understand how the App is used and to diagnose crashes and errors. Firebase Privacy.
  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may use the advertising identifier and other data to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. AdMob & Privacy.

These providers may collect and process data as described in their respective policies. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the data practices of these third parties.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App and its gameplay.
  • To analyze usage and performance, and to fix bugs and crashes.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the App free.

The App stores your game progress (such as high scores, achievements, collected items, and den layout) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us.

4. Advertising & Your Choices

Ads served by AdMob may be personalized based on your advertising identifier. You can opt out of personalized advertising or reset your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android). Where required by law (e.g., for users in the European Economic Area and the UK), the App requests consent before serving personalized ads.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their policies. To request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App, contact us at the email below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
